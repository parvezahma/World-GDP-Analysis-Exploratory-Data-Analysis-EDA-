Key Insights

Global GDP demonstrates a long-term upward trend with identifiable periods of slowdown.

A small group of countries contributes a disproportionately large share of total global GDP.

GDP values are highly right-skewed, reflecting global economic inequality.

Emerging economies show accelerating growth patterns in recent decades.

These insights are exploratory in nature and intended to guide further economic modeling or forecasting.
